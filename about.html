Our Mission

Biotech Innovation Group (BIG) is dedicated to pioneering scientific research and driving innovation.

The company's origin stems from a vision of resourcefulness, initially specializing in cell-based assays and developing computational solutions to support biological research and diagnostics, within and beyond the borders of Pakistan. Additionally, BIG is broadening its focus to include the production of essential biological molecules, notably proteins, to lower operational expenses.

BIG aims to reflect its evolving expertise by fostering a culture of efficiency, innovation, and sustainability.
