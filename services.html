Our Services (Wet lab)

To aid your drug discovery efforts, we offer the following cellular assays/experiments:

  • Proliferation assays (SRB, MTT, MTS, ADP Glo etc.) for drug evaluation (Screening, IC50s etc.)
  • Apoptotic assays (Annexin PI staining, PARP and caspase 3/7 cleavage)
  • Degradation assays for PROTACs
  • Immunofluorescence
  • 3D growth of spheroids and colony formation
  • Western blotting
  • Drug combination studies
  • Gene expression analysis through q-PCR
